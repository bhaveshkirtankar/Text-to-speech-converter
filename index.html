How To Use TalkGPT 4 Easy Steps

Step 1

Type or paste your text into the box provided or upload a file (.txt).

Step 2

Choose your preferred voice from the dropdown list of available options.

Step 3

Click the “Generate Speech” button to hear your text spoken aloud instantly.

Step 4

To stop playback, click the button again. Adjust and enjoy seamless speech conversion!
